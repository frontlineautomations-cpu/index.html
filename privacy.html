Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

Frontline Automations (“Frontline”, “we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, business name, and phone if provided).
  • Message content you include in forms (e.g., notes about what you want automated).
  • Basic usage data (e.g., logs/analytics) if enabled by our hosting/provider tools.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information or demos.
  • To deliver scheduling, reminders, and service updates if you opt in to SMS.
  • To improve our services and site performance.

SMS consent and texting

If you opt in to receive SMS from Frontline Automations, message frequency may vary and message/data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

We do not sell or share SMS consent with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information with service providers (e.g., SMS/telecom platforms) strictly to deliver messages and operate our services.

Data sharing

We may share information with trusted vendors that help us operate (email/SMS delivery, hosting, analytics), under appropriate confidentiality and security terms.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information.
  • You can opt out of SMS anytime by replying STOP.
